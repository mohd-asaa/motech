Who We Are

A startup built on
purpose and principle

MOHD stands for More Healthy Dynasty. We are not just a technology company — we are a movement rooted in ethics, community, and the belief that health in all its dimensions — physical, economic, environmental — is a fundamental right.

  • Founded and based in Tanzania, serving Dar es Salaam and Zanzibar
  • Operating across three interconnected and mutually-reinforcing verticals
  • Partnering with individuals, SMEs, NGOs, and institutions to scale impact
  • Driven by ethics-first business practices in every decision and initiative
  • Committed to accessibility, inclusion, and long-term community wellbeing
Our Three Pillars

Where we operate,
and why it matters

Each vertical is purposefully chosen — together they form a complete, self-reinforcing system for building healthier lives, healthier communities, and healthier economies.

EdTech

Education &
Health Training

Expanding Opportunity Through Knowledge

We make learning about health scalable, personalized, and accessible to all. By partnering with individuals and organizations, we bring health education to communities that need it most — removing barriers and building awareness one training at a time.

  • Scalable health training programmes
  • Personalized and community-adapted curricula
  • Multi-organizational and institutional partnerships
  • Digital and in-person facilitation models
  • Train-the-trainer frameworks for wider reach
AgriTech

Food Systems &
Nutrition Supply

Strengthening What Sustains Us All

Food is health. We strengthen food production, sustainability, and the supply chain of essential nutritions. Our AgriTech work supports farmers, suppliers, and distributors to build a resilient, health-focused food ecosystem for Tanzania and the region.

  • Sustainable food production support
  • Nutritional supply chain resilience
  • Farmer and supplier capacity building
  • Technology-enabled agricultural access
  • Gulf–Tanzania produce trade facilitation
FinTech

Business Growth &
Financial Tools

Building Access, Trust, and Scale

Financial inclusion is health too. We improve access, trust, and efficiency in the financial system by offering ethical business opportunities — along with the tools and training needed to scale businesses to heights previously unimaginable for many Tanzanians.

  • SME and NGO business development support
  • Financial literacy and business training
  • Digital tools for retail and business management
  • Grant writing and fundraising consultancy
  • Islamic finance (Mudarabah, Musharakah, Murabaha) advisory

Our Values

Ethics is not an
afterthought — it is our foundation.

Every decision, partnership, and offering at MOHD Technologies is filtered through a lens of deep ethical commitment. We believe that sustainable prosperity and principled conduct are not in tension — they are the same thing.

01
Health First
Every product, service, and partnership is evaluated on its contribution to genuine human health and wellbeing. Profit never comes at the cost of people.
02
Transparency
We operate with full honesty in our dealings, pricing, partnerships, and impact reporting. Trust is our most valuable asset and we protect it fiercely.
03
Accessibility
We actively dismantle barriers to health knowledge, nutrition, and business opportunity — especially for underserved and marginalized communities.
04
Sustainability
Our decisions account for long-term impact on communities, environment, and economies — not just quarterly returns. We build for generations, not quarters.
05
Partnership
We believe no organization succeeds alone. We pursue genuine, mutual-benefit partnerships with individuals, SMEs, NGOs, and institutions at every level.
06
Islamic Finance
Where financial structures are involved, we apply principles of ethical Islamic finance — Mudarabah, Musharakah, Murabaha — ensuring fairness and shared risk.
07
Empowerment
Our goal is never dependency — always capability. We train, equip, and mentor so that individuals and communities build lasting self-sufficiency.
08
Legacy
We are building a dynasty — for our communities, our families, and the generations who will inherit this world. Every act of business is also an act of stewardship.
